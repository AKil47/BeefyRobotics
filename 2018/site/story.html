Our Story

How it all began

The Incident

One day our founder and CEO Sarah Habib (affectionately referred to as Sarah Bear) was walking among a coast and discovered a turtle who had unfortunately passed away due to local pollution in the area. That day, she found her purpose. Within the following weeks, her and her group of friends created Atlantech, a company that leverages the innovations and advancements of the 21st century to put a stop to the atrocity that is ocean pollution. After a solemn funeral for the turtle, named Beefy by the group, Atlantech got to work. It is with great pleasure that just a few years later, Atlantech has grown to be on the forefront of innovation and on the front lines of fighting the war against pollution.

Our Mission and Values

AtlanTech is a creative, innovative robot manufacturing company aimed at offering high-quality, moderately priced robots to sustain the cleanliness of our oceans. We partner with our customers, our employees, our community and our environment to create global, inventive solutions to help reach its full potential.
